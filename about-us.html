Meet Our Dietitian

Dietitian Sukriti is a dedicated health professional with a combined background in pharmacy, nutrition, yoga, meditation, spirituality, and well-being. Her journey in holistic wellness began with a Bachelor's in Pharmacy from National Model College for Advanced Learning, Kathmandu, followed by a Master's in Nutrition and Dietetics from the College of Applied Food and Dairy Technology, Lalitpur. Sukriti believes in a holistic approach to nutrition, emphasizing whole foods and mindful eating practices to support clients in achieving their health goals through evidence-based nutrition. She believes that food is medicine, and by making a conscious dietary choice, individuals can optimize their well-being and prevent disease. She runs various diet and nutrition related training programs online as well.

Area of Expertise

Her areas of expertise include, holistic nutrition and diet planning for weight loss, diabetes and heart health, digestive disorders, diabetes management.
